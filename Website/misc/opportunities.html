Technology Opportunities

COIVD and Online Dating

In today's age, online dating presents an opportunity to the members of society to make meaningful relationships even while being isolated from one another due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Online dating allows people who wish to start a relationship to do so instantly, and without having to put themselves or the other person at risk during the pandemic.

Introverts and Online Dating

Introverts are natrually quiet people who prefer the comfort of silence to the comfort of people, meaning that most introverts would feel more comfortable talking to other people behind a screen, as there is less pressure without another person being present. With the introduction of online dating, introverts can talk and start relationships with other people in a more comfortable mannner for them, as the talking is done through a screen. This also means that as soon as the introvert feels comfortable with the other person, they can organise to meet in real life, and carry out a conversation comfortably in person (hopefully).
